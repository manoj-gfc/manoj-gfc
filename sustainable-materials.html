Future of Architecture: Sustainable Materials

Published on April 25, 2026 | By Manoj Kumar

Why Sustainable Materials Matter

Eco-friendly materials like bamboo, recycled steel, and low-carbon concrete are revolutionising the construction industry. They reduce environmental impact, improve energy efficiency, and often cost less in the long run.

Top 5 Sustainable Materials for 2025

  • Cross-laminated timber (CLT) – strong, renewable, and carbon-sequestering.
  • Recycled plastic bricks – lightweight and durable.
  • Hempcrete – excellent insulation and breathability.
  • Ferrock – made from waste steel dust, stronger than concrete.
  • Mycelium composites – grown from mushrooms, fully biodegradable.
